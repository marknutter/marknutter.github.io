Athas is by far the cruelest world TSR has created. Athas is a world where water is scarce and steel is worth it's wait in gold. Foul new creatures have been born to Athas, creating an unforgiving envirenment to those who attempt to survive. Powerfull sorcerer kings, and enormous city-states lie on this scorched world of sand and silt. By mine, and many other's opinions, Dark Sun is by far the most exciting world TSR has created.
